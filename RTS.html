RTS stands for real time strategy which is the oppitsite of a TBS which is a turn-based strategy. Both are types of sub genres of strategy games Instead of taking turn after turn, all of the players play a one time. So the game does not progress by taking turns. This is personally my favorite type of game.Some of the most famous RTS games include Age of Empires, Command & Conquer, Warcraft, Starcraft, and Company of Heroes just to name a few of them.
